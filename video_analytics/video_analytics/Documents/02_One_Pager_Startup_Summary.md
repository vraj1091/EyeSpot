# EyeSpot Video Analytics: Enterprise Start-up One-Pager

## **Company Overview**
EyeSpot Video Analytics upgrades existing, passive CCTV networks into an active, intelligent security guard. Using state-of-the-art vision models (YOLOv8 + ByteTrack), we analyze behavioral intelligence across 100+ concurrent feeds, specifically targeting weapon detection, shoplifting, and operational compliance (masking, unauthorized access, phone usage) in real time.

## **The Problem**
- **Astronomical Retail Shrink:** Brick-and-mortar stores lose $100B+ globally to theft.
- **Action Inefficiency:** Humans watching 16+ feeds simultaneously lose focus after 20 minutes, rendering live monitoring useless.
- **Forensic vs. Preventive:** Existing cameras only provide recordings *after* an incident has occurred.

## **Our Solution**
EyeSpot transforms these cameras into proactive AI systems without requiring new hardware.
1. **Real-time Detection:** Weapon and shoplifting identification with sub-second dashboard alerts.
2. **Behavioral Analytics:** Detect unauthorized gathering, active loitering, queue buildups, and staff absence.
3. **Centralized Command Structure:** A highly responsive React-based dashboard and API platform to alert security teams instantaneously on mobile devices or control rooms.

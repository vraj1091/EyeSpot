## Slide 3: The Solution
**Proactive. Autonomous. Intelligent.**
EyeSpot Video Analytics transforms existing IP camera networks into an autonomous AI guard that detects threats instantaneously.
* **Real-time Threat Identification:** Weapons, shoplifting gestures, masking, and unauthorized access.
* **Behavior Intelligence:** Advanced pipeline tracking suspicious loitering, queue buildups, and employee absences.
* **Instant Alerts:** Sub-second latency alerts (visual, sound, platform, and webhooks).
* **Zero New Hardware Required:** Our inference engine hooks directly into your current RTSP camera feeds.

## Slide 4: How It Works (The Tech Pipeline)
* **Ingestion:** High-performance RTSP stream processing capable of 100+ concurrent cameras per distributed shard.
* **Edge/Core AI Engine:** Running optimized YOLOv8 architectures combined with ByteTrack for persistent multi-object tracking.
* **Behavior Policies:** Zonal logic rules (e.g., employee absense trigger, discussion/gathering zones, loitering).
* **Actionable Output:** Pushed through WebSockets to a modern React dashboard & webhook system for security teams.
